Welcome to Mow Better Lawn Care

After years in the lawn care industry we've become familiar with most of the challenges that come between you and a healthy lawn. We've also seen the common pitfalls and avoidable mistakes that come with a lack of experience, attention to detail, and poor communication.

Experience and a focus on customer service means staying ahead of the challenges, avoiding surprises, and keeping your lawn care simple. Dependability and attention to detail means a worry free experience you can count on.

Knowing better is mowing better!

Sharp Blades

Did you know dull blades shread and damage grass making it more susceptible to disease? Our guaranteed, routine blade sharping means a clean cut and a healthier lawn for you.

Clean Equipment

Did you know weed seeds and lawn disease can spread from lawn to lawn by hitching a ride on the mower deck? Especially if you hire a mowing company that doesn't clean their mower deck after each lawn. Cleaning our mower decks between every lawn, means we're keeping your lawn clean, healthy, and disease free.
